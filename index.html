Barnold's drawing pad ✏️

-------------------------HOW TO USE:-------------------------

-------------------------Colour controls-------------------------

R: Increases red in the RGB scale for pen colour

G: Increases green in the RGB scale for pen colour

B: Increases blue in the RGB scale for pen colour

E: Decreases red in the RGB scale for pen colour

F: Decreases green in the RGB scale for pen colour

V: Decreases blue in the RGB scale for pen colour

Space: Overrides the colour to a preselected colour

-------------------------Pen and pad controls-------------------------

🡺: Increases pen size

🡸: Decreases pen size

🡹: Increases how much a colour can increase by for the RGB scale

🡻: Decreases how much a colour can increase by for the RGB scale

C: Clears the page

N: Removes the black outline from shapes

M: Add the black outline to shapes

S: changes the pen shape
